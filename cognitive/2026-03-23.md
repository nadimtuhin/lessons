# Cognitive — March 23, 2026

### Skill
**Working Memory**
This is your "mental scratchpad"—the ability to hold and manipulate information in your mind for short periods. It is crucial for complex tasks like following instructions, problem-solving, and reading comprehension.

### Exercise
**The Reverse Span Task**

Do not use a pen or paper. Read the following sequence of digits **once**. Close your eyes and hold them in your mind for 5 seconds. Then, open your eyes and type the sequence in **reverse order**.

**Sequence:**
`8` `4` `2` `9` `1` `7` `5`

*Example: If the sequence was 1-2-3, you would type 3-2-1.*

### Reflection
**Question:** While you were holding the numbers, did you "chunk" them into smaller groups (e.g., 842 and 9175) to help you remember, or did you try to recall them individually? Which strategy felt more effective?

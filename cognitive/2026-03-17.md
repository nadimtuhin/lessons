### **Skill: Inhibitory Control (Executive Function)**
Training this ability improves your capacity to suppress impulsive responses and ignore distractions. It is essential for emotional regulation, sustained attention, and making high-quality decisions under pressure.

### **Exercise: The Color Interference Test**
Read the following list of words **out loud** as fast as you can. However, you must say the **color of the text**, not the word itself.

1. **BLUE**
2. **RED**
3. **GREEN**
4. **BLUE**
5. **RED**
6. **GREEN**
7. **BLUE**
8. **RED**

*(Did you hesitate? Your brain’s automatic reading instinct competed with your visual processing.)*

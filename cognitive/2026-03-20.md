# Cognitive — March 20, 2026

### 1. Skill: Working Memory
Working memory is your "mental sketchpad"—it allows you to hold and manipulate information in your mind over short periods. Strengthening this ability improves focus, complex problem-solving, and comprehension.

### 2. Exercise: The Visual Reverse
Read the following sequence of words. **Do not write them down.** Close your eyes and hold them in your mind for 10 seconds. Then, open your eyes and type the words **in reverse order** (from last to first) without looking at the list.

**Sequence:**
*   Ocean
*   Candle
*   Galaxy
*   Whisper
*   Velvet
*   Ladder

*(Wait 10 seconds with your eyes closed before typing.)*

***

### 3. Reflection
Did you lose your place in the sequence, or did the words visually shift while trying to reverse them? What strategy (chunking, visualization, repetition) helped you maintain accuracy?

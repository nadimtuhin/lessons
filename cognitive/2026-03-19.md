# Cognitive Skill: Working Memory
**Why it matters:** Working memory is your mental "scratchpad." It allows you to hold and manipulate information in your mind over short periods. It is fundamental for problem-solving, following instructions, and reading comprehension.

### The Exercise: The Reverse Countdown
Perform the following mental calculation **without** using a pen, paper, or calculator. Do this entirely in your head.

1.  **Step A:** Start with the number **1,000**.
2.  **Step B:** Subtract **7**.
3.  **Step C:** Keep subtracting 7 from the new result.
4.  **Step D:** Continue this process until you reach a number below **800** (or calculate 10 consecutive steps).

***

### Reflection
Did you lose your train of thought or feel your mind "drift" during the calculation? If so, at roughly what number did it happen?

# Cognitive — March 16, 2026

### 1. Skill: Working Memory
This exercise trains your **verbal working memory** (the phonological loop). It matters because it allows you to hold and manipulate information in your mind for short periods, which is essential for reading comprehension, following instructions, and meaningful conversation.

### 2. Exercise: Reverse Span
In this exercise, you must hold a list of words in your mind and recall them in the exact reverse order.

1. **Read** the following list of words **one time only**. Do not write them down.
2. **Close your eyes** and count backward from 10 to 0 silently to clear your immediate focus.
3. **Recall** the list **backwards** (starting with the last word and ending with the first).

> **Word List:** Thunder, Mirror, Balloon, Whisper, Engine

### 3. Reflection
Did you lose the order of the words, or did you lose the words entirely? (This indicates whether the failure was in *sequencing* or *storage*).

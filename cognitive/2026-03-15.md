# Cognitive — March 15, 2026

### 1. Skill: Working Memory
**Why it matters:** Working memory is your cognitive "scratchpad"—it allows you to hold and manipulate information in your mind over short periods. It is fundamental for problem-solving, following instructions, and learning new things.

### 2. Exercise: The Cognitive Juggle
Read the following list of items exactly **once**. Do not write them down. Look away and wait 30 seconds. Then, on a piece of paper, write down the list in **reverse alphabetical order**.

**The List:**
*   Apple
*   Detective
*   Umbrella
*   Notebook
*   Lizard

*(Hint: First, organize them alphabetically in your head A-Z, then flip them.)*

***

### 3. Reflection
**Did you find yourself "chunking" the words together or mouthing the words to help you retain them?**
*(Understanding your internal compensation strategies reveals how you naturally bypass cognitive limits.)*
